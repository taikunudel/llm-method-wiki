Statistician at the University of Southern Queensland. Co-author with Gordon Smyth of the `tweedie` R package and the foundational series-evaluation (2005) and Fourier-inversion (2008) methods for Tweedie densities. Application work spans rainfall, fishery, and insurance modeling. His PhD work focused on numerical methods for densities without closed form.
