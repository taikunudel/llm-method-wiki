Danish statistician (1954–2015). Established exponential dispersion models as a field of study (1987, 1997). His 1997 monograph "The Theory of Dispersion Models" is the standard reference for the [[TweedieDistribution]]. With his student de Souza, introduced the application of Tweedie compound-Poisson models to insurance claims data — the basis the Smyth–Jørgensen 2002 DGLM extension builds on.
