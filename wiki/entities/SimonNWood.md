Statistician at the University of Bath (now Edinburgh). Author of the `mgcv` R package — the de facto standard for GAM fitting. Author of "Generalized Additive Models: An Introduction with R" (2nd ed. 2017), the standard reference. His 2011 paper ([[wood-2011-gam-reml]]) defines the full-Newton REML smoothness selection method that makes [[GeneralizedAdditiveModels]] practical for real applications.
