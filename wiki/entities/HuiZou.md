Statistician at the University of Minnesota. Co-developer of the elastic net (Zou & Hastie 2005) and the adaptive lasso (Zou 2006) — foundational variable-selection methods that underpin both `HDtweedie` ([[qian-2016-hdtweedie]]) and many other high-dim regression tools. Long-time collaborator with Yi Yang and Wei Qian on Tweedie-family extensions.
