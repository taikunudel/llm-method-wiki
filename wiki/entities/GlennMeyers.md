Actuary and researcher at ISO Innovative Analytics. Long-time contributor to the Casualty Actuarial Society. Co-author of the ordered [[GiniIndex]] framework for evaluating insurance pricing models ([[frees-meyers-cummings-2011-gini]]). His work focuses on practical model validation methodology — bridging statistical theory and rate-filing requirements.
