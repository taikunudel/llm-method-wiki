Statistician at the Rochester Institute of Technology. Co-author and lead of the `HDtweedie` R package ([[qian-2016-hdtweedie]]) for high-dimensional Tweedie regression with grouped elastic net. Also co-author on the `TDboost` R package ([[yang-2016-tdboost]]). His work focuses on computationally efficient algorithms for penalized GLMs.
