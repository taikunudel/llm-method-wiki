Actuary and researcher at SGH Warsaw School of Economics, Institute of Econometrics. Co-author with Mathias Lindholm and Mario Wüthrich on the 2021 paper reconciling Poisson-Gamma vs Tweedie EDF parametrizations and extending the comparison to neural networks.
