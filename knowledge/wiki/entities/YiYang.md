Statistician at McGill University. Lead author of the `TDboost` R package ([[yang-2016-tdboost]]) for gradient tree-boosted Tweedie regression. Co-author on `HDtweedie`. Research focus is high-dimensional statistical learning, with applications in actuarial science.
