Actuary and R developer. Author of the `cplm` R package — the canonical implementation of Tweedie compound-Poisson linear (and mixed) models in R, shipping the standard `AutoClaim` benchmark dataset. His 2013 paper ([[zhang-2013-cplm]]) derives the Laplace/AGQ/MCEM/MCMC estimation routines that the package implements.
