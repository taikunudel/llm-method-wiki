Professor of Actuarial Science at the University of Wisconsin–Madison and consultant to ISO Innovative Analytics. Author of "Regression Modeling with Actuarial and Financial Applications" (2010), the standard graduate textbook for actuarial regression. Co-developer of the *ordered* [[GiniIndex]] and [[LorenzCurve]] for insurance pricing ([[frees-meyers-cummings-2011-gini]]). His work bridges academic statistics and practical industry pricing.
