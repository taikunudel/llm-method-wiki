Professor at RiskLab, ETH Zurich. One of the most prolific contributors to modern actuarial science, with major works on stochastic claims reserving, non-life insurance pricing, and the application of machine learning (especially neural networks) to insurance problems. Co-author of [[delong-lindholm-wuthrich-2021-tweedie]] arguing for Poisson-Gamma over Tweedie EDF, particularly for neural network extensions.
