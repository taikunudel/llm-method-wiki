Statistician at the Walter and Eliza Hall Institute of Medical Research (Melbourne). Author of the `statmod` and `tweedie` R packages — the foundational implementations behind every Tweedie-family pricing model in this wiki. Best known for the Tweedie distribution numerical evaluation work with Peter Dunn ([[dunn-smyth-2008-tweedie-fourier]]) and DGLM-based dispersion modeling for insurance ([[smyth-jorgensen-2002-tweedie-dispersion]]). Also active in bioinformatics (limma).
