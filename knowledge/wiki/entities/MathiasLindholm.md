Statistician at Stockholm University, Department of Mathematics. Co-author with Łukasz Delong and Mario Wüthrich on the 2021 review of Tweedie parametrizations and their neural-network extensions. Research includes non-life insurance modeling and statistical learning for actuarial applications.
